Our Services

Explore our comprehensive range of fertility and multispecialty medical services designed to meet your needs.

In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

Our In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) program is tailored to help couples overcome fertility challenges. Using cutting-edge reproductive technology, our experienced fertility specialists provide personalized treatment plans, including ovarian stimulation, egg retrieval, fertilization, and embryo transfer. We offer advanced techniques like Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) and Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT) to maximize success rates. Our compassionate team supports you through every step, ensuring emotional and medical care of the highest standard.

Antenatal Care

Our Antenatal Care program provides comprehensive support for expectant mothers throughout pregnancy. We offer regular check-ups, ultrasound scans, nutritional counseling, and prenatal screenings to monitor the health of both mother and baby. Our team of obstetricians and midwives ensures early detection of potential complications, personalized birth plans, and education on labor and delivery. With a focus on comfort and safety, we strive to make your pregnancy journey smooth and stress-free.

Book Appointment
Neonatal Care

Our state-of-the-art Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is equipped to provide specialized care for newborns, particularly premature or critically ill infants. Our NICU features advanced technology, including incubators, ventilators, and continuous monitoring systems, staffed by expert neonatologists and nurses. We offer round-the-clock care, nutritional support, and developmental assessments to ensure the best outcomes for your baby. Our family-centered approach keeps parents informed and involved at every stage.
